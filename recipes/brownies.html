Brownies


Best brownies ever!


These brownies make your mouth water, and make you convince yourself, "just one more!". The recipe is fairly easy to make, and doesn't take long!
Just throw some ingredients into a bowl, mix, sprinkle on some walnuts, and in the oven it goes; making your house smell delicious!

Prep time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cups walnus halves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F)
  3. Mix all ingredients, except for the walnuts
  4. Spread the batter into a prepared pan
  5. Decorate with walnut halves
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry and the edges start to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20-30 minutes
  7. Cool before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
